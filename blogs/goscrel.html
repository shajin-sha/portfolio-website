GoScrel - Ride hailing platform 🚗

GoScrel is an ride hailing platform that is not jest like Uber or Ola, it is more than that. It is an ride hailing platform that connects passengers and drivers and even booking bus tickets, track bus and get live updates.

Shajin KP

20th June 2021



Build by whom?

GoScrel is completely build by me(Shajin KP) for SCREL INFO PVT. LTD. I Took care of all backend and frontend


Why GoScrel

1. Commission free 💰

2. All in one platform 🚗

3. Book bus tickets 🚌

4. Track local and reserved KSRTC buses 🚌

5. Get live updates of buses or set an alarm to get notified when bus is near to your stop 🚍

6. Book cabs 🚗

7. Book auto 🛺


Technologies used

1. Flutter for IOS and Android

2. NodeJS for backend

3. MongoDB for database

4. Nodejs + Socket.io for live updates

5. 3rd party APIs Maps and navigation

Flutter with provider for state management and Freezed for data classes.

Authantication method?

Mobile & OTP


Team work or individual work?

Individual work (Only me)


Top features of GoScrel

1. Accurate Fare Estimation based on factors like time, distance, and vehicle type. Know exactly what you'll pay before you start your journey.

2. Book bus tickets and track bus and get live updates or even set an alarm to get notified when bus is near to your stop

3. Book cabs and auto
